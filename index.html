About Mr. Hong

Welcome to Mr. Hong,

Through our menu, explore the diversity of Chinese & South East Asian cultures and join us as we celebrate our heritage with an offering of the most authentic flavours of Asia.

Functions

For a function to remember and an experience that you will be proud to have hosted you need to contact Mr. Hong to discuss our options.

Our function menus offer something for everyone, and requests can be discussed with our team to ensure your function is tailored to your needs. Mr. Hong can cater for groups of all sizes, from 20 to 350 guests. We offer several unique spaces and exclusive venue packages.

Menus

At Mr. Hong, we have created a designated kitchen area for preparation and cooking of our vegan menu to avoid contact of non-plant products at all time. We pride ourselves as one of the very few Asian restaurants in Australia to have no added MSG in our foods.

We are also very proud of our extensive gluten-free & dairy-free selections, offering real choices that valued market. Your thirst is also well catered as our Sommelier and Mixologist have developed an amazing drinks menu to complement everything that is Mr. Hong.
